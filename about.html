About the Zine

Our Goal:

Now I don't know if things have changed much in the education system, but from what I remember, rarely were we ever introduced to women in STEM.

We want to make up for that.

Every month we will release an issue profiling a woman who made significant contributions to different STEM fields, with the hope that our readers will learn something new! At the end of the year we hope to release a print issue with all of the 12 women from that year, which readers can order from our website under the “Order” tab.

Subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated on our monthly issues, and follow us on our socials to receive hints about who our issue focuses on! We're happy to have you here, and hope you stick around a while :).
