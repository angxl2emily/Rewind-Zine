Rosalind Franklin: Who really discovered the shape of DNA?

When we learn about DNA in high school biology, the names that usually come up are Watson and Crick. What we don't usually learn is the name of the woman behind their discovery--her name was Rosalind Franklin.

Ava Lovelace: The First Computer Programmer

Despite the huge gender disparity in the tech industry today, the first published computer algorithm was written by a woman: Ada Lovelace. Using Charles Babbage's analytical engine (which is basically the first computer), Ada Lovelace developed an algorithm to compute Bernoulli numbers.
